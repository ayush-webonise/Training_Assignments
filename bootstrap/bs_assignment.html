WHAT WE DO?

MicroSave is a diversified development consultancy that works to better the life of low-income people.

Established in 1998 as a joint UNCD-F/DFID project in East and Southern Africs, today we have offices and staff in 24 countries around the world with headquaters in Lucknow, India.
